Burnside Digital

  • Intern / Contract Developer
  • 10/2012 - 03/2013

I was originally hired as an intern as part of what would become the Portland Code School. I was kept on as a Contract Developer until the company was sold. In my time at Burnside Digital, I worked on a medical application and a movie community site.

The medical application was charting software that allowed doctors to create template charts and fill them in with a speech-to-text service. The app was split into a Backbone.js frontend and a Rails 4 backend. Working on a team of four developers, my major contribution was a drag-and-drop system for nesting sub-sections of the template.

During a redesign of the movie community site, I was put on a team with two designers. I made any changes to the rails backend as needed, beefed up the test suite, managed the team's git workflow, and deployed releases.
