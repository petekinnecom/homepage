Study of Mathematics

Story

As the son of an engineer, mathematics has always come naturally to me. Excelling in Calculus 1 & 2 in high school, it wasn't until college that my abilities were really challenged. As an undergrad, I learned that I have the frustrating inability to let go of a difficult mathematics question. Although stressful at times, graduate school served to both pique my curiosity and satisfy my desire to understand. I routinely dream up new theories and make sure to always have a puzzler in mind in the event I have to wait in line.
